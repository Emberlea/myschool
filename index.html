Oakridge Schools in Top 10 EducationWorld Rankings

Oakridge International schools receive top rankings in the recently announced EW India School Rankings for the year 20-21. Education World Rankings is the world’s largest and most in-depth school ratings and rankings survey based on field interviews with more than 11,368 knowledgeable stakeholders (parents, teachers, principals, educators and senior students) in school education countrywide. This year, over 2,000 of India’s most high-profile schools have been surveyed and have been evaluated on 14 different parameters ranging from Academic Reputation, Curriculum delivery, Facilities, Faculty competence, to Student achievements in Academic and Extra-curricular areas. Oakridge International school Gachibowli featured yet again in the Top 10 International schools in India. This is the 4th consecutive year that Oakridge Gachibowli has featured among the 10 Best International schools in India. Oakridge Gachibowli is also among the best international schools in Hyderabad and ranked No 1 in the state.





Oakridge Schools are in ‘Top 5’ in the national school rankings survey – Times of India

Oakridge International Schools have once again ranked in the top 5 schools in the annual Times School Rankings for 2020-21. We have been consistently ranking as one of the top international schools in India and have an exemplary national and international reputation for our outstanding academic excellence. From innovative educational opportunities to quality infrastructure, we provide high standards of teaching methodology, technology collaboration, and global culture in every aspect of learning. The recent TOI survey saw the participation of parents, principals, educationalists and senior students across the nation to rate the country’s top 1,000 schools. We are delighted to secure top positions in this prestigious survey for the best international schools in all cities.





Oakridge International School Mohali ranked No.1 Day School in Mohali and Punjab by EducationWorld rankings

Oakridge International School Mohali is ranked No.1 International Day School in Mohali and Punjab by The Education World rankings 2020-21. For the 5th consecutive year, the school is among as the Best International School in Mohali and Chandigarh.
